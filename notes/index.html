About this format

These notes use a collapsible outline format. Each section can be expanded or collapsed by clicking its heading. The idea is that you can scan the structure first and then drill into what interests you, rather than reading linearly.

There is also a toggle to hide external links, if you want to focus on the content without the references. The notes themselves are a distillation of reading and experience, not original research. The references are there so you can go deeper on anything that catches your attention.
